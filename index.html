Skill Into Rural India

We believe human talent is evenly distributed across socioeconomic demographics; opportunity to develop that talent is not. The Prifenaa Cheritable Trust aims to help bridge the gap. We provide opportunities for life-changing education to students in rural India, and require their accountability and mentorship back to others in their communities in exchange. We believe in the 'hand up' and not 'hand-out' charitable model, and welcome all who share our beliefs in fundamental human dignity, equality of opportunity and social accountability to join us in our joyful work.

Our Mission

To empower communities through education, cultural preservation, and sustainable development initiatives that create lasting positive impact on society. We strive to bridge educational gaps, promote social equity, and foster an environment where every individual can reach their full potential.

Our Vision

A world where quality education, cultural heritage, and community development opportunities are accessible to all, regardless of economic background or geographical location. We envision thriving communities built on knowledge, compassion, and sustainable practices.

Playscape at Koduru Elementary school - March, 2019
Play is the best education and highest form of research & playgrounds are spaces that help children build the physical, mental and emotional tools to live a healthy life. -Dr.Meena Veluri who funded this project

Profenaa Charitable Trust spearheaded the transformational playscape project in March 2019. The design of all-weather, eco-friendly playground has been drawn up by Anthill Creations, a not for profit organization which mobilizes communities to build all-inclusive and sustainable playscapes. We have used recyclable material like scrap tires, cable & oil drums etc. to create these beautiful playscapes. The adaptive reuse of scrap is both economical and sustainable with a start to end construction timeline of less than 4 weeks and cost under Rs.2.5 Lakhs.
