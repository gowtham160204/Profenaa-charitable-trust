Education Program

Skill Into Rural India

At Profenaa Charitable Trust, we believe that education is the foundation for social progress and individual empowerment. Our Education Programs are designed to uplift underprivileged children, youth, and marginalized communities by providing equal access to quality learning opportunities.

Objectives

Bridge the gap between privileged and underprivileged learners.

Support children from economically weaker sections to pursue schooling.

Encourage higher education for first-generation learners.

Provide life skills and digital literacy to make students future-ready.

Key Initiatives

1. School Support Program

We assist children from low-income families with learning materials, school fees, uniforms, and guidance to prevent school dropouts.

2. Scholarship & Sponsorships

Special scholarships for deserving students, especially orphans, single-parent children, and girl students, ensuring continuity in their education.

3. Skill-Based Education

Short-term courses in computer basics, digital skills, communication, and vocational training to empower youth for employability.

4. Awareness & Mentorship Camps

Workshops on career guidance, health awareness, and value-based education to nurture holistic development.

5. Adult & Community Education

Literacy programs for adults and women to encourage lifelong learning and community growth.
