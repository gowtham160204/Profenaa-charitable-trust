Skill Into Rural India

Areas of Training

Computer & Digital Literacy

  • Basic computer operations and MS Office applications
  • Internet usage and online services navigation
  • Introduction to digital tools for education and employment
  • Cyber safety and responsible internet usage

Advanced Computer & Technical Skills

  • Programming basics (C, Java, Python)
  • Web designing and development
  • Digital marketing and e-commerce fundamentals
  • Data entry and IT-enabled services

Vocational Skills

  • Tailoring, handicrafts, and traditional skills
  • Spoken English and communication development
  • Personality development and soft skills
  • Industry-specific vocational training

Entrepreneurship & Self-Employment

  • Guidance on small-scale business development
  • Financial literacy and access to government schemes
  • Mentoring for start-ups and self-help groups
  • Business plan development and implementation

Social Impact

Equal Access

Providing equal access to learning opportunities for rural and urban poor, breaking down barriers to education and skill development.

Reduce Unemployment

Reducing unemployment through skill-based training that directly addresses market demands and creates job opportunities.

Economic Independence

Enabling women and youth to achieve economic independence through sustainable skill development and entrepreneurship support.

National Development

Supporting national development by creating skilled manpower and contributing to the country's economic growth and competitiveness.
